# Marketing

 * [EquiTrend The Harris Poll](https://theharrispoll.com/equitrend/) [1](https://theharrispoll.com/equitrend-ranking-page/); [2](https://theharrispoll.com/the-harris-poll-announces-this-years-brands-of-the-year-2020/):
