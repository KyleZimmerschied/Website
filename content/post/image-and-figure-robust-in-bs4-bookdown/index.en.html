I struggle to find how to make my figures and images perfectly aligned in the center of my book. After looking around, I think I’ve found an adequate solution:

Step 1: Change your image into % format.

Step 2: I typically change my width to 100, but you can also leave it as is

Step 3: Go to Attributes tab can put this line into the “CSS styles” box: display: block; margin: 1em auto

Voila! Now your image or figure should be robust to both bs4 or gitbook styles.
